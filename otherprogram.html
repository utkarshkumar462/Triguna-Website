P2E Program

Passport to Earning Program Orientation

This initiative is an e-learning solution designed to empower India’s young people with relevant 21st-century skills, preparing them for success in school, work, and life. In collaboration with B-Able Foundation, our Passport to Earning (P2E) India initiative offers free, job-relevant skills training to young individuals aged 14-29.

Mapped to the National Education Policy 2020 (NEP), P2E offers a wide range of curated courses, including Life Skills, Digital Literacy, Financial Literacy, Employability, and Leadership. These courses, available in Hindi and English, are certified by United Nations, UNICEF, and P2E partners including Microsoft, Accenture, and Capgemini.

To date, we have successfully certified over **100,138+ students** across **16 states** and **60+ districts** in India, reaching **600+ schools, colleges, and institutions**.
