Our Initiatives

“DON’T TRY TO DRIVE THE HOMELESS INTO PLACES WE FIND SUITABLE. HELP THEM SURVIVE IN PLACES THEY FIND SUITABLE.”

Team Triguna Organized an EYE Care Camp in shelter home of Earth Savior Foundation. Camp was facilitated with the help of CLIO Eye care Gurugram. We were able to provide vital eye care services to mentally retarded people, psychiatric illness and senior citizen people living in shelter home of Earth Savior Foundation. The success of the Eye Camp was a testament of belief in our mission and your commitment to making a positive difference in the lives of others. Total of 291 eye examinations and screenings, provided counselling support and necessary medicine. 60 odd patients was prescribed with spectacles and was facilitated with spectacles. Team Triguna is thankful for your support directly contributed to these outcomes, and the smiles on the faces of those who benefited from your kindness that were truly heartwarming.
