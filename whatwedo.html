What We Do

Education

Education is the transformative power to uplift individuals and communities. Our educational programs aim to break down barriers of socio economic backwardness and create opportunities for the lesser privileged members of society. Through scholarships, faculty development training, and infrastructure development, we create conducive environment for children and have access to quality education. We prioritize digital literacy, equipping youth with essential computer skills and internet proficiency to thrive in the digital age. Our vocational training and education programs are designed to impart learning such that it enhances the practical skill and employability of the beneficiaries. Education is for one and all, thus the importance of adult literacy, offering Team Triguna Adult literacy modules aims at benefitting adults who may not have had formal education the chance to learn new skills and achieve functional literacy. Team Triguna strives to empower individuals to secure better jobs, start businesses, and contribute to the overall economic development of their communities at grassroots level.

Livelihood

We recognize the importance of livelihood programs in uplifting marginalized communities and promoting economic empowerment. These programs are designed to address the barriers faced by individuals in accessing education, resources, and networks, which often hinder their ability to secure employment or start their own businesses. Through vocational training, entrepreneurship development, microfinance support, and value chain development, we aim to equip individuals with the necessary skills and knowledge to pursue self-employment and entrepreneurship. Our goal is to enhance livelihood opportunities, improve economic conditions, and empower communities to become self-sufficient. By fostering sustainable livelihoods, we strive to create a pathway towards a brighter and more prosperous future for all. We work towards building the capacity of the community members to plan, implement, and manage their livelihood initiatives

Healthcare

Each and every human being should have access to basic healthcare, regardless of their socio-economic background. We strive to improve healthcare services in underprivileged areas by setting up medical camps and partnering with reputed entities that provide general and specialized medical facilities. Our programs aim to promote awareness and prevention of health-related issues, addressing public health challenges at the grassroots level. Prevention is key, and through education and outreach, we empower communities to take control of their health. Team Triguna with its medical outreach modules bridges the healthcare gap between the beneficiaries and the medical facilities, thereby ensuring equal opportunities to the beneficiaries to lead a respectable healthy life.

Vocational Training

In today’s rapidly evolving world, there exists a significant gap between the skills imparted by formal education systems and the dynamic requirements of the industry. Recognizing the immense potential of young people as the backbone of our nation, we understand the need to bridge this gap through skills development programs. Our focus lies in equipping students with a comprehensive skill set that aligns with the current demands of the industry. By offering vocational training programs, we empower young individuals to acquire the skills that are in high demand in the job market. Additionally, we foster an entrepreneurial spirit among them, providing opportunities to explore and establish their own businesses. Moreover, we recognize the importance of digital literacy and offer training to ensure young people can actively participate in the digital economy. Through these initiatives, we aim to unlock the potential of youth, enabling them to thrive in today’s competitive landscape and contribute to the growth and development of our society.
